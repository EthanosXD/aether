How it works

Three steps to a
free internet

No accounts. No configuration. Just install and connect.

01

Install Aether

One command installs the Aether node on your machine and starts it as a background service automatically.

02

Join the network

Your node finds other Aether nodes automatically — on your local network and across the internet — with no manual setup.

03

Browse freely

Point your browser's proxy to localhost:1080 and your traffic flows through the Aether network.

Features

Built different

No blockchain. No tokens. No middlemen. Just a network that works.

🌐

No ISP Required

Devices connect directly to each other. In areas with enough nodes, Aether can replace your ISP entirely.

🔒

Censorship Resistant

Traffic is distributed across hundreds of nodes. There's no single point to block, filter, or shut down.

⚡

Automatic Discovery

Nodes find each other automatically on your local network and across the internet. Zero configuration.

🆓

Free Forever

No subscriptions, no tokens, no hidden fees. Aether is free to use and open source.

🔓

Open Source

Every line of code is public. Audit it, contribute to it, or run your own piece of the network.

🖥️

Runs Anywhere

Any Linux machine can be an Aether node — laptop, Raspberry Pi, old desktop, cloud server.
